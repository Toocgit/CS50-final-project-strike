Nutrition

Total Daily Energy Expenditure

Your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is a best estimate, given some of your body measurements and activity levels, of the calories you burn per day. Your TDEE is a measure of your maintenance calories, which are the calories you can consume daily and maintain your current body weight.

There are easy to use TDEE calculators on the web, a Google search will find many. Three are linked below.

  • tdeecalculator.net
  • iifym.com/tdee-calculator
  • mytdee.com

Be aware that a TDEE is only a best estimate maintenance calorie level. We may need to adjust, which we'll come back to.
